Z22 Iron Balls

A high conviction Bitcoin strategy with systematic overlays.

BTC BTC Yield Fund Monthly
Category Bitcoin
Tags
BTC BTC Yield Fund Monthly
Description
Z22 Iron Balls is a high-conviction Bitcoin strategy that keeps a permanent BTC core while applying systematic overlays to actively harness volatility and compound purchasing power across market cycles. The approach blends macro, on-chain, and sentiment analysis to determine the optimal mix of overlays at any given time. These include systematic trend-following to capture directional momentum in BTC and select liquid cryptocurrencies, systematic option selling to monetize elevated implied volatility, cross-exchange arbitrage to exploit pricing inefficiencies in perpetual futures, and volatility premium harvesting to accumulate BTC during distressed markets. By dynamically combining these strategies around a constant Bitcoin base, Z22 Iron Balls seeks to turn Bitcoin’s volatility from a challenge into a long-term growth engine.
